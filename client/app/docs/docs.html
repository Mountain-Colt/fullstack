Researcher Documentation


What You Need:

A parallelized algorithm or MapReduce that we can run on our cluster.


What We Provide:

We integrate your algorithm into our system, promote it through our application, and drive users toward your project so that you obtain the valuable computing power that you need.



What types of projects are ideal:

At this point we are pretty flexible - scientific or mathematical projects are the best fit, but if you believe our platform would be of assistance in getting users to contribute to your project, please sign up and contact us.
