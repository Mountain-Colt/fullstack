HOW IT WORKS

Your computer spends most of its time sitting idle.

Contribute that idle processing power
to research projects of your choice.

Crowd Crunch maintains a cluster of master nodes.
Your computer joins the cluster as a worker node.
The masters can then distribute individual computational tasks to each worker.

When you download our mac OSX app, a worker node in a box will live on your computer.
Don't worry--it's entirely contained and separate from your system.
Nothing inside can get out.

That worker will be available to contribute computing power to the cluster
according to the schedule you specify in the app settings.

GETTING STARTED

1. Download the mac OSX app.

  • Download App

2. Install the App. You may need to temporarilly adjust your security settings to allow our app to install, becasue we are net yet a registered Apple developer. See the steps in this article.

3. Select a project to which you'd like to contribute.

4. Adjust your preferences to set the schedule of when to allow your computer to contribute.
