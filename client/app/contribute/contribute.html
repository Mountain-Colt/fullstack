HOW IT WORKS

Your computer spends most of its time sitting idle.

Contribute that idle processing power
to research projects of your choice.

Crowd Crunch maintains a cluster of master nodes. Your computer joins the cluster as a worker node. The masters can then distribute individual computational tasks to each worker.

When you download our app, a worker node in a box will live on your computer. Don't worry--it's entirely contained and separate from your system. Nothing inside can get out.

That worker will be available to contribute computing power to the cluster according to the schedule you specify in the app settings.

GETTING STARTED

1. Download the app.

  • Download App

2. Select a project to which you'd like to contribute.

3. Adjust your preferences to set the schedule of when to allow your computer to contribute.
