You're not using your computer all the time.

We provide researchers with access to that idle processing power.


HERE'S HOW IT WORKS...

Crowd Crunch maintains master nodes. You can think of a master node as akin to mission control. Its job it to guide and direct the airplanes it oversees, or in our case, its worker nodes.


When you download our app, one of those workers will live on your computer.


Based on your preferences, that worker will be available to contribute computing power whenever specified.


Our job at Crowd Crunch is to take large-scale projects and break them up into smaller, more manageable chunks.


Then, once those jobs are sufficiently parallel, the Crowd Crunch master node is able to divvy up those tasks amongst its workers.


As a result, you're able to contribue raw computer-processing power to worthwhile projects, right away.
